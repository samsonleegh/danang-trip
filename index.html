D1

Thursday 14 May

Arrive + Con Market + Beach + Evening Stroll
🗺️ View Day 1 route on Google Maps
1:10 PM

① Arrive Da Nang Airport 📍

Clear immigration (Singapore passport = visa-free 30 days). Grab to hotel — only 10 min.

1:45 PM

② Check in — Golden Sand House 📍

Drop bags, freshen up, regroup. Near My Khe Beach.

2:30 PM

③ Con Market (Chợ Cón) 📍

Skip touristy Han Market. 2,000+ stalls, authentic, where locals actually shop. Fresh produce, street food, clothes, Vietnamese coffee. Banh xeo, mi quang, skewers all under SGD 3.

42 Luong Ngoc Quyen St. Cash only. Google Translate camera is your friend.

4:30 PM

④ My Khe Beach 📍

First swim! Wide white sand, gentle waves. Sunbeds VND 50–100k. Golden hour photos.

6:30 PM

④ My Khe sunset + beachfront stroll

Golden hour on the beach. Walk along the coastal promenade — seafood restaurants, coconut stalls, and beach bars light up as the sun sets.

7:30 PM

⑤ Dinner — Bac My An or An Thuong 📍

Both walkable from hotel. Bac My An = cheapest local street food. An Thuong = cafes, craft beer, international mix. VND 60–120k/pax.

D2

Friday 15 May

Ba Na Hills + Massage + Dragon Bridge Fire Show 🔥
🗺️ View Day 2 route on Google Maps
7:00 AM

① Breakfast & depart for Ba Na Hills

Hotel breakfast at Golden Sand House. Driver departs (~45 min drive). Arrive by 8 AM when park opens.

Buy tickets on Klook in advance. ~950,000 VND (~SGD 55) incl. cable car + Fantasy Park.

8:30 AM

② Cable car + Golden Bridge 📍

One of Asia's longest cable cars (20–25 min, 360° views). Head to Golden Bridge first — best photos before 10 AM.

If foggy, do Fantasy Park first and return to the bridge when it clears.

9:30 AM

② French Village & Gardens

Cobbled streets, cathedral, town square. Le Jardin d'Amour. 5–10°C cooler than Da Nang.

Older folksCafe in French Village. Browse shops. Flat and easy. Enjoy the cool mountain air.
Younger crewFantasy Park — alpine coaster, roller coaster, 4D rides, horror house, 90+ arcade games. All included.
12:30 PM

② Depart Ba Na Hills

Cable car down. Back at hotel by 1:30–2 PM.

2:30 PM

③ Spa & massage

90-min full body massage. Herbal Spa or Queen Spa. ~SGD 12–20/pax. Perfect post-mountain wind-down.

7:00 PM

④ Dinner — Banh Xeo Ba Duong 📍

Best crispy pancakes in Da Nang. Hidden alley — go to the one at the END. VND 30–50k/serving.

K280/23 Hoang Dieu, Hai Chau. Use Google Maps to find the alley.

8:30 PM

⑤ Dragon Bridge Fire Show 🔥 📍

Arrive by 8:30 PM for a good spot. Show at 9:00 PM sharp — dragon breathes fire and sprays water for ~15 min. Free. Spectacular!

Stand on east bank (Bach Dang St) for best view. Don't stand too close — water spray reaches far!

9:15 PM

⑥ Son Tra Night Market supper 📍

Right at the foot of Dragon Bridge. Perfect post-show spot. Grilled seafood, skewers, banh xeo, smoothies. Electric atmosphere.

D3

Saturday 16 May

Lady Buddha → Marble Mountains → Hoi An Lanterns ✨
🗺️ View Day 3 route on Google Maps
8:00 AM

① Breakfast & check out Golden Sand House

Pack up. Luggage in van for the day.

9:00 AM

② Son Tra — Linh Ung Pagoda (Lady Buddha) 📍

67m statue. Panoramic coastal views. Free entry. Flat grounds. ~45–60 min.

10:30 AM

③ Marble Mountains 📍

Older folksElevator up (VND 15,000). Huyen Khong Cave + pagodas at base. Flat, accessible. Skip summit. ~1 hr.
Younger crewFull stairs to summit — 360° views! Am Phu Cave (extra VND 40k). Steep but rewarding. 1.5–2 hrs.

Entry VND 40,000 (~SGD 2.50)

12:30 PM

④ Seafood lunch + Lotte Mart 📍

Be Man Seafood or local Mi Quang. Optional Lotte Mart stop for packaged gifts (fixed prices, air-con).

2:30 PM

⑤ Drive to Hoi An — check in Coco Việt Homestay 📍

~30–40 min drive. Check in by 3:30 PM. Rest up for the evening.

5:00 PM

⑥ Hoi An Old Town — first exploration 📍

Old Town ticket VND 120,000 (5 heritage sites). Japanese Covered Bridge, Fujian Assembly Hall. Flat, car-free, shaded.

5:30 PM

⑥ Lanterns at sunset ✨

Hundreds of silk lanterns light up the entire old town. Walk along Thu Bon River. Release a floating lantern (VND 10–20k). Saturday = best lantern night of the week.
7:00 PM

⑦ Hoi An Night Market dinner 📍

Riverside: grilled skewers, banh xeo, spring rolls. Or Morning Glory Restaurant for Cao Lau + White Rose dumplings. VND 50–150k/pax.

D4

Sunday 17 May

Coconut Village + Cycling + An Bang Beach
🗺️ View Day 4 route on Google Maps
9:00 AM

① Cam Thanh Coconut Village — basket boats 📍

Round basket boats through coconut palm waterways. Boatmen sing and do spinning tricks! Fun for all ages, gentle. ~1.5 hrs. VND 150–200k/pax.

Wear sunscreen + hat. Bring waterproof phone pouch.

11:00 AM

② Banh Mi Phuong 📍

Anthony Bourdain's favourite. Queue moves fast. Or try Madam Khanh. VND 30–50k.

12:00 PM

③ Cycling — rice paddies + Tra Que Village 📍

Whole group cycles through lush rice paddies to Tra Que Vegetable Village. Flat terrain, easy pace, beautiful scenery. Bike rental ~VND 30–50k or guided tour ~VND 200k/pax.

Rent bikes from hotel or old town. Route is flat and shaded — suitable for all ages.

2:30 PM

④ An Bang Beach 📍

Quieter beach near Hoi An. Sunbeds VND 75k. Swim, relax, beach bars. ~20 min from old town.

5:30 PM

⑤ Sunset in Hoi An 📍

An Hoi Bridge or rooftop bar. Lanterns equally beautiful on Sunday.

7:00 PM

⑥ Farewell group dinner 📍

Splurge: Mango Rooms or Vy's Market Restaurant. Budget: Night Market again. Or The Field (rice paddy fine dining).

9:00 PM

⑦ Last night — Thu Bon River 📍

Lantern boat ride on Thu Bon River (VND 50–100k). Bar-hop. Final massage. Soak it in.

D5

Monday 18 May

Morning Hoi An → Airport → Home
🗺️ View Day 5 route on Google Maps
7:30 AM

① Early morning Hoi An

Old town in morning light — quietest, best photos. Japanese Bridge without crowds.

9:30 AM

① Last shopping

Collapsible lanterns, Vietnamese coffee, silk scarves, Hoi An spices.

10:45 AM

② Check out Coco Việt & depart

Driver to Da Nang Airport (~45 min). Arrive by 11:30 AM.

2:15 PM

③ Fly home

Arrive Singapore ~5:30 PM. Home for dinner!

Riverside + lanterns. Saturday = best atmosphere of the week.

Hoi An Night Market

Second visit — try different stalls. Or Hoi An Central Market area for a local vibe.
Eat: Com ga, sweet treats, different stalls

Alternative local picks Helio Night Market — Da Nang's largest. Food, beer, seafood, and shopping zones. Near the Sun Wheel. Wide aisles, great for groups. Open 5:30 PM–10:30 PM daily. ~15 min Grab from My Khe.

Le Duan Night Market — Small, local, zero tourists. Budget clothes, accessories, and street food along Le Duan St. Popular with Da Nang locals. Open 6 PM–11 PM.

Practical tips

VisaNot required — Singapore passport holders get visa-free entry for up to 30 days.
CurrencyVietnamese Dong (VND). VND 10,000 ≈ SGD 0.55. Vietcombank/BIDV ATMs. Cash for markets.
Transport16-seat van with driver booked. Grab for short trips. Da Nang–Hoi An = 30–40 min.
Weather (May)Dry season. 30–35°C. Ba Na Hills 5–10°C cooler. Sunscreen + hat essential.
Dragon BridgeFire show every Fri/Sat/Sun 9 PM. We catch it Friday (Day 2). Arrive 8:30 PM.
MarketsCon Market (local) over Han Market (touristy). Lotte Mart for packaged gifts. Bac My An for street food near hotel.
Night marketsSon Tra (at Dragon Bridge, best after fire show). Helio (largest, food + beer + shopping). An Thuong (walkable from hotel). Hoi An Night Market (riverside + lanterns). Note: Thanh Khe Tay & Hoa Khanh may be closed — skip these.
For older folksEverything flat. Elevator at Marble Mtns. Cable car at Ba Na. Basket boats gentle. Hoi An car-free.
